#### 解释：看product数量相同否

## 代码

````sql
```mysql
SELECT customer_id 
FROM Customer 
GROUP BY customer_id
HAVING COUNT(distinct product_key) = (
  SELECT COUNT(product_key) FROM Product
);
````
