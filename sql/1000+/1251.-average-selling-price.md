## 代码

```sql
select p.product_id, round(sum(u.units * p.price) / sum(u.units), 2) as average_price
from Prices p
left join UnitsSold u
on p.product_id = u.product_id and (u.purchase_date between p.start_date and p.end_date)
group by product_id;
```
